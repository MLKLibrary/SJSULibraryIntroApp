Printing

You can print from any library computer, USB, or laptop. Printing stations can be found on the lower level, 1st floor, 2nd floor, and 5th floor.

Mezzanine

The Mezzanine is an SJSU student-only space for collaborating, studying, or just relaxing between classes. It is located between the lower level and 1st floor.

Laptops and iPads

Free checkout of PC laptops, MacBook Pros and iPads using your SJSU Tower Card on the Mezzanine and 4th floor.

Laptops and iPads (Website)

Student Computing Services

Student Computing Services provides students free access to PC laptops, MacBooks and iPads. They are located on the 4th floor.

Student Computing Services (website)

Library Cards

A library card and PIN are needed to borrow items or get access to ebooks and databases. Bring your Tower Card to the Circulation Desk to get a library card.
